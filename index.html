About the Club

We are a student-led club focused on creative collaboration: web & app design, films, photography, and tech projects. We run workshops, showcases, and hack-events to build, learn, and grow together.

Purpose

  • Learn practical skills (design, dev, production).
  • Collaborate on real-world projects.
  • Showcase work & get feedback.
